# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

Justice Companion exists to help those who cannot afford legal representation. We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free, supportive experience for everyone, regardless of:

- Economic status or background
- Age, disability, ethnicity, gender identity, or sexual orientation
- Level of experience or education
- Nationality, personal appearance, or religion
- Any other aspect of identity

## Our Standards

### Positive Behaviors We Encourage:

- **Empathy First** - Remember many users are in difficult legal situations
- **Accessibility** - Make features usable by everyone
- **Clear Communication** - Use plain language, avoid legal jargon where possible
- **Respectful Collaboration** - Value all contributions
- **Privacy Protection** - Respect and protect user data
- **Constructive Feedback** - Help others improve their contributions

### Unacceptable Behaviors:

- Discriminatory language or actions
- Harassment, trolling, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information
- Dismissing the struggles of those seeking legal help
- Any conduct that could harm vulnerable users
- Providing unauthorized legal advice (we provide information, not advice)

## Our Responsibilities

Project maintainers will:
- Clarify standards of acceptable behavior
- Take appropriate action against unacceptable behavior
- Remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this code
- Ban temporarily or permanently those who violate these standards

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies:
- Within project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- When representing the project publicly
- When interacting with users seeking help

## Enforcement

Report unacceptable behavior by:
1. Opening a private issue on GitHub
2. Contacting project maintainers directly
3. Using GitHub's reporting features

All complaints will be reviewed promptly and fairly.

## Remember Our Mission

This project exists because the justice system often fails those without resources. Every contribution, no matter how small, helps someone access their legal rights. Let's work together with compassion and respect.

## Attribution

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the Contributor Covenant, with special considerations for our legal aid mission.
